Hardwear Tools

    Screwdriver
    • A tool with a shaped tip for turning screws.
    • Types include flathead and Phillips for different screws
    • Used in assembling furniture and electronics.
    • Magnetic tips on some screwdrivers hold screws in place.
    • Essential for precision tasks requiring screw fastening.

    Hammer
    • A tool with a heavy head for driving nails or breaking materials.
    • Includes types like claw hammers for pulling nails
    • Used in carpentry, construction, and metal shaping
    • Ergonomically designed handles provide control and reduce fatigue.
    • Safety is essential when using to avoid accidents.

    Wrench (Spanner)
    • Used to grip and turn nuts and bolts
    • Available as adjustable wrenches for multiple sizes
    • Common in automotive and plumbing work.
    • Prevents damage to fasteners when used properly
    • Socket wrenches allow quick size changes

    pliers
    • A hand tool for gripping, twisting, and cutting wires.
    • Comes in various types, like needle-nose and cutting pliers.
    • Common in electrical work and detailed craftwork
    • Ergonomic handles improve comfort and precision.
    • Used in both professional and DIY projects
